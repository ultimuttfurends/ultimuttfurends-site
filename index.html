Meet Deepa

Meet Deepa

Your dog’s new best friend.

As a dog sitter, my top priority is making sure your pets feel happy, safe, loved, and cared for while you’re away. I pay close attention to the little details that matter — their food, routines, potty breaks, environment, walking areas, favorite activities, and anything they should avoid.

I treat every pet like they’re my own, with patience, affection, and personalized care. Whether your dog loves morning walks, belly rubs, fetch, swimming, brushing, puppuccinos, or just cozy companionship, I take the time to bond with them in a way that feels familiar and comforting.

You can count on me to be honest, respectful, reliable, and communicative. I provide regular updates, plenty of love, and thoughtful care from start to finish. I also treat every client’s home with the same respect I’d want for my own, so you can come back to a clean, stress-free space.

My goal is simple: to give you peace of mind and become your pet’s new favorite friend while you’re away.
