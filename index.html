Meet Deepa

Your dog’s new best furend.

As a dog sitter, my top priority is making sure your pets feel happy, safe, loved, and cared for while you’re away. I pay close attention to the little details that matter — their food, routines, potty breaks, environment, walking areas, favorite activities, and anything they should avoid.

I treat every pet like they’re my own, with patience, affection, and personalized care. Whether your dog loves morning walks, belly rubs, fetch, swimming, brushing, puppuccinos, or just cozy companionship, I take the time to bond with them in a way that feels familiar and comforting.

You can count on me to be honest, respectful, reliable, and communicative. I provide regular updates, plenty of love, and thoughtful care from start to finish. I also treat every client’s home with the same respect I’d want for my own, so you can come back to a clean, stress-free space.

My goal is simple: to give you peace of mind and become your pet’s new favorite furend while you’re away.

FAQ

Good things to know.

What holidays are subject to holiday pricing?

Holiday pricing applies to any drop-in visit, walk, or entire overnight stay that falls on or includes one of the following holidays: New Year's Day, Valentine's Day, St. Patrick's Day, Easter, Memorial Day, Independence Day, Labor Day, Halloween, Thanksgiving, Christmas Eve, Christmas Day, and New Year's Eve.

When does the puppy rate apply?

The puppy rate applies to dogs under 2 years old. It may also apply to younger dogs that require additional attention, training reinforcement, frequent potty breaks, or a higher level of care than an average adult dog.

Does the senior rate apply to all older dogs?

Not at all. The senior rate is only applied when a dog requires extra assistance or specialized care, such as incontinence management, mobility assistance, frequent bathroom support, age-related medical conditions, or increased supervision.

What is considered a last-minute booking?

A last-minute booking is any reservation made less than 48 hours before the requested service. For holiday periods, a last-minute booking is any reservation made less than 72 hours before the service begins.

When does the after-hours rate apply?

My normal operating hours are 8:00 AM – 8:00 PM. Services scheduled outside of these hours may be subject to an after-hours surcharge.

What is included with overnight pet care?

Every overnight stay includes a detailed daily report, 5–10 photos and videos each day, food and water bowl cleaning, mail and package collection, trash and recycling takeout, plant watering within reason, playtime, two 15-minute walks per day, regular potty breaks, basic medication administration, light housekeeping, pet waste pickup, pet toy organization, and lots of love.

Will I receive updates while I'm away?

Absolutely. Communication is one of the most important parts of pet care. You'll receive regular updates, photos, and videos so you can relax knowing your pet is happy, safe, and well cared for.
